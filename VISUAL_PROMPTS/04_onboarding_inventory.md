Warm and energetic — a new day starting at the store.

BOTTOM: Lower 30% fades to cream gradient.

STYLE: Semi-realistic, rich detail, warm Indian grocery context, burnt orange + cream + 
product colors. Premium Bakaloo-level quality. Full canvas, no margins.
