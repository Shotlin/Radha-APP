> This locks the style so all 10 illustrations feel like one brand.

## Style Lock

**App:** RADHA — Indian retail audit & store management app (like Blinkit for store owners)
**Style:** Premium semi-realistic digital illustration. Anime-influenced warmth and expressiveness
with realistic proportions. NOT cartoon/chibi. Think: Bakaloo app illustrations crossed with 
editorial-quality character art.

**Character:** One consistent protagonist — a young Indian male store owner/manager (mid-20s, 
short wavy black hair, light stubble, warm skin tone, confident posture). He appears in EVERY 
illustration in different contexts/emotions. He is the store owner the app serves.

**Color palette:**
- Primary accent: Burnt orange #EA580C (the RADHA brand — kurta color, UI elements, highlights)
- Canvas warmth: Cream #FFFBF5 (never pure white)
- Ink: #1C1917 (never pure black)
- Supporting: Warm terracotta, turmeric gold, fresh greens from vegetables
- Mood: Warm, golden-hour lighting, soft bokeh, Indian urban warmth

**Environment:** Modern Indian kirana/grocery store — clean, organized, colorful product shelves.
Real recognizable Indian products on shelves (Parle-G, Amul, Maggi, Tata Salt, etc).
Urban Gujarat/India visible through windows — apartment buildings, warm sunlight.

**Composition rules:**
- FULL BLEED — illustration fills 100% of canvas, edge-to-edge. NO white margins.
- Portrait orientation (9:16 mobile aspect ratio)
- Character occupies 60-70% of the frame
- Bottom 30-40% fades to a soft warm gradient (where the app UI card will overlay)
- Products and environment fill the remaining space — NO empty areas
- Soft focus/bokeh in background for depth
- Warm golden lighting throughout

**What NOT to do:**
- No white/empty space anywhere
- No flat/vector style — this is rich, painted, detailed
- No generic Western characters — this is specifically Indian
- No cluttered text or UI elements in the illustration
- No dark/moody lighting — always warm and optimistic
- No cultural stereotypes (no garba dancers, rangoli as decoration, etc.)
